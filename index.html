young island is a beautiful private island retreat that is a 2 minute boat ride from st vincent.the 13-acre resort offers 29 villas set in a verdant garden with an assortment of amenities,including a swin up bar,spa kalinda and nearby 19th century rock fort (fort duvernette).

Botanical Gardens

Video edited on Kapwing

The Botanical Garden is one of the most important and popular sites in St Vincent. it is the oldest botanical garden in the Western Hemisphere (established in 1765) and is also an important conservation site (particularly for the St Vincent parrot). The Botanical Garden is a short journey from the town centre of Kingstown.

La Soufriere Volcano

la Soufriere is a stratovolcano with a crater lake and is the islands youngest volcano.visitors can view the volcanic crater by following a hiking trail that ascends through the rainforest to the rim.
